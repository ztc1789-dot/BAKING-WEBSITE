The story behind us:

Lucky twist was born out of frustration for the lack of east asia food and atmosphere I used to crave when I first moved to Barcelona

After some cinsiderable "market research", aka, eating my way through Hong Kong and South East Asian countries (including Vietnam and Indonesia), I came up with the first idea for Lucky Twist.

In our cafe you can find some of the most cheerished and loved baked treats and refreshing drinks inspired by my months long sabbatical travelling in that regions.
